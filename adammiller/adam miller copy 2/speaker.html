Speaker

Adam is a talented speaker and motivator, extremely knowledgeable in business, promoting products and services to individuals as well as major companies. He has a humorous, charismatic charm that captures his audience’s attention. He inspires his listeners to strive to do their best and to brings out a contagious energy that moves people into action.

In addition to business meetings, team motivational seminars and DVD’s, Adam devotes some of his time to Drug Rehabilitation programs encouraging teens and young adults to choose other more productive alternatives to drug addiction and often talks openly about the value of proper Diet, Nutrition, Rest, and Exercise.
