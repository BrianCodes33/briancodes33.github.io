Adam Vincent Gilmer

Advisor

Utilizing his years of experience in the network marketing and the direct sales industries, Adam has served as consultant to several companies, providing expertise to create revenue and solve problems. He has also negotiated the terms and conditions of products and services or personal contracts between productive individual networks and the corporate sides of their prospective companies.

Adam has served on the board of directors or has been an advisor for more than a dozen companies as well as non-profit organizations. As a managing partner in a contract management consultancy firm Adam’s focus is in designing strategic cash flowing models and growth plans for the execution of this model.

This includes: world-class acquisition of leadership, development of personnel, the assessment of challenges and opportunities in foundational core business principles and practices, strategic innovation, joint venture development, sales and marketing best practices, corporate culture, customer acquisition campaigns, customer loyalty and retention systems and more.
